"We can create a better future if we aim for the best."



Yamanan comes from the tagalog word "Kayamanan" which means "treasure" in the english language. We decided to use Yamanan as a name for our project since it is a very rare name and we believe that it will thrive soon. This is all about helping each other in building success for the future and training oneself to become a competent professional. In fact, anyone who wants to attain his/her profession must experience great hardwork and practice. The more skillful a person is, the more opportunities will come. Big companies commonly hire high-skilled workers for performance improvement and production boost which helps the business grow even more than what they have expected. We encourage people to turn his or her expectations into reality where they can be what they want to be and to also improve oneself performance and deeds.


Yamanan is for young entrepreneurs or small business establishments where they can learn how to advertise, promote, and as well as negotiate. We aim to help start up businesses expand their company and creat on their own. This website is not popular yet the reason for making this project is to help wanna-be entrepreneurs and advertisers to become what they want to be. In this content, you are freely able to showcase your designs and ideas with no limit and to discover different kinds of designs made by other users. If you like to start something, it is best to understand and acquire the foundation first before doing anything. Create a straight plan, set a goal, negotiate, invest, and build up. These are the things you should consider before starting something big. Think like your planting a seed and that it will soon grow in the right time and develop into something convenient for yourself, for your community, or rather for your country.




Success comes for a price and that is hardwork. No one can be successful for free but it is paid with hardwork, time, determination, decision, and effort. What I mean hardwork is that you will encounter several challenges which will test your skill, knowledge, and your mindset. These challenges may lead to so many trials and failures but it doesn't hinder you to be successful in the future. Tasks- these are the challenges and works that you have to pass in order to accomplish your goal.

Comply every task and output you have completed and observe your performance's improvement. Keep track of your works and put them all together.

Tasks Instruction and Guidance

Here are the instructions of each task:

Mini-task #1

The task was all about making a visual representation out of the given statement "I love Chicken" by using any kind of an editing software.This is the easiest task among the other tasks distributed since representing something on your own understanding or perspective isn't hard. The more effort you exert, the more creative you will be.

Mini-task #2

In this task, we were expected to select a still or steady photo of ourselves and make different scenes out of it by using any kind of editing software. This task was a little bit hard to do especially for the first-timers who weren't able to experience this kind of activity.(Sorry, Attachment not available due to technical problems.)

Mini-task #3

This task is all about Photography. We have to take a photo of ourselves in one of our favorite area at home and then use any kind of editing software to make our photo even more impressive. In this generation, People who pose effortlessly and still looks attractive in photographs are called photogenic. It would be unchallenging for them to do this task.

Mini-task #4

This task starts to be a little bit of challenging because you have to be creative in doing it. It is all about advertising your product or making a PRINT-AD using any available editing software. Advertising your product in a significant and an eye-catching way would be a great help with the business you're working on. Opportunities and negotiations will rise when you advertise your product effectively.

Mini-task #5

It still talks about advertising, but this is different from the recent task you have read. In this task, you have to make a Point-of-Purchase(POP) Material for your product. POP Materials are marketing tools used for advertising your products or it could be merchandising. POP Materials could be standees and banners that you can see in the market or the billboards that you have seen on the street, or many other ways of advertising.Big companies Advertise their products on televisions, social medias, and other websites since it is more effective.

Mini-task #6

It is still related with Mini-task #5 information but in this task you will be doing a Point-of-Purchase(POP) Display for your product. POP displays are also marketing materials placed beside the merchandise you're promoting. Shelves are one of the examples of POP Displays.
